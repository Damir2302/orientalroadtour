Samarkand

Samarkand! One of the oldest cities in the world, the cradle of world civilization, the capital of ancient Sogdiana and the empire of Amir Temur, the same age as Rome, Thebes and Athens. "Eden of the Ancient East", "Face of the Earth", "Pearl of the Muslim World" - this is how ancient and modern authors call Samarkand.

Bukhara

Sacred Bukhara - this is how the Uzbeks respectfully call this glorious city. An ancient belief says: a blessed light descends from the sky to all Muslim cities, and only over Bukhara it rises to the sky.
